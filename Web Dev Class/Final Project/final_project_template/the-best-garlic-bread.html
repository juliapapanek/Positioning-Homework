The Best Garlic Bread


STEPS

Ingredients

5 Tbsp Earth Balance

1 Tbsp finely chopped garlic

1 Tbsp finely chopped parsely

1 Tbsp nutritional yeast

1 lb load of bread



GET STARTED


STEPS

Step 1

Preheat oven to 350 degrees and melt Earth Balance in the microwave for about 20 seconds in a small bowl.



STEP 2


STEPS

Step 2

Add garlic, parsley, nutritional yeast, salt and pepper and stir to combine.



STEP 3


STEPS

Step 3

Cut the loaf of bread in half lengthwise and spread garlic butter evenly on the two halves.



STEP 4


STEPS

Step 4

Bake in the oven for about 10 minutes then toast under the broiler for about 2 minutes, or until crispy and golden brown. Serve immediately or freeze once cooled.
